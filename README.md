# Saints of Avilund

A tabletop role-playing game (TTRPG) with a planned companion video game adaptation.

---

## What This Is

Saints of Avilund is a low-fantasy TTRPG set in a richly detailed world with no standard fantasy races, no all-powerful gods, and no comfortable certainties. Players are human — always — and the world they move through is medieval in feeling but uneven in development: one city feels like London in 1750, a province to the south feels like 1350, and the far north is 1050. All three exist at once.

The supernatural is real. Magic happens. Saints may or may not hear your prayers. But none of it is spectacular. It is quiet, unsettling, and occasionally terrible.

The world is discovered through the documents that people in it actually wrote — journals, pamphlets, church records, field notes, letters — not through a narrator explaining it to you.
