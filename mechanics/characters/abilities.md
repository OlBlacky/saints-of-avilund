# Abilities

## Ability Types

Every ability belongs to one of four types:

| Type | Usage | Pool | Refresh |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Basic** | Available to all characters at any time | — | — |
| **Class Standard** | Class-specific, usable any time | — | — |
| **Class Special** | Class-specific, limited use | Special Pool | Short Rest |
| **Class Main** | Class hallmark abilities, limited use | Main Pool | Long Rest |

### Basic Abilities
Available to every character regardless of class. Examples include Melee Attack and Ranged Attack.

### Class Standard Abilities
Can be used any time with no restriction. Examples: Fighter Power Strike, Wizard Arcane Bolt.

### Class Special Abilities
Draw from the Class's **Special Pool**. Each use costs 1 pool point. The pool resets on a **Short Rest** (to be defined).

- Starting Special Pool = 1 (granted when you purchase the Class)
- Each class has its own independent pool
- Pool can be increased with AP (see [Advancement Points](advancement-points.md))

### Class Main Abilities
Draw from the Class's **Main Pool**. Each use costs 1 pool point. The pool resets on a **Long Rest** (to be defined).

- Starting Main Pool = 1 (granted when you purchase your first Main ability)
- Each class has its own independent pool
- Pool can be increased with AP (see [Advancement Points](advancement-points.md))

## Ability Categories

Each Class has 2 primary Ability Categories. Abilities within those categories are cheaper to purchase and improve. There are 12 categories:

| Category | Description |
|---|---|
| **Arms** | Combat, weapons, fighting techniques |
| **Survival** | Endurance, hardship, staying alive |
| **Nature** | Wilderness, animals, the natural world |
| **Stealth** | Hiding, deception, subterfuge |
| **Social** | Persuasion, manipulation, influence |
| **Academics** | Knowledge, lore, study |
| **Divine** | Faith, holy power, sacred calling |
| **Spiritual** | Inner discipline, mind over body |
| **Primal** | Raw instinct, savage power, ancient forces |
| **Arcana** | Studied or innate arcane power |
| **Occult** | Forbidden knowledge, dark pacts |
| **Craft** | Making, building, inventing |

## Ability Qualities

Every ability is defined by the following qualities:

| Quality | Description |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Basic / Class Standard / Class Special / Class Main |
| **Category** | One of the 12 categories above |
| **Activation Time** | Standard Action, Full Round Action, or minutes (non-combat) *(name TBD)* |
| **Range** | Distance the ability reaches |
| **Area of Effect / Targets** | How many targets or what area is affected |
| **Attacking Attribute** | Which attribute's Attack Bonus is used |
| **Defending Attribute** | Which attribute's Defence Target is used |
| **Damage** | Fixed value or weapon-based |
| **Effects** | Ongoing Damage, attribute impairment, Charmed, Dazed, etc. *(full list TBD)* |
| **Duration** | How long the ability lasts |

### Attacking & Defending Attribute Costs
- Attacker can choose from multiple attributes → **more expensive**
- Defender can choose from multiple attributes → **less expensive**
- Attacker forces the defending attribute → **more expensive**

### Weapon-Tied Abilities
Some abilities inherit their damage from the equipped weapon rather than a fixed die. For example, a Fighter's Power Strike uses the weapon's damage die plus modifiers such as +Str.

### Damage AP Costs
| Damage | AP Cost |
|---|---|
| 1 (fixed) | 1 |
| 1d4 | 2 |
| 1d6 | 3 |
| 1d8 | 4 |
| 1d10 | 5 |
| 1d12 | 6 |
| 1d20 | 10 |

### Duration AP Costs
| Duration | AP Cost |
|---|---|
| Instant | Base (no extra cost) |
| 1 round (6 seconds) | +2 AP |
| Longer durations | Scale up *(full table TBD)* |
| Non-combat durations (minutes/hours) | Discounted *(rate TBD)* |

## Standard Abilities by Category

These are example Class Standard abilities. Each class has access to abilities from its 2 categories.

### Arms
1. **Power Strike** — a melee weapon attack that adds your Str bonus to damage
2. **Defensive Strike** — a melee attack that grants +1 to your Defence until the start of your next turn
3. **Disarming Strike** — a melee attack that attempts to knock the opponent's weapon away (Str attack vs Str Defence)

### Survival
1. **Endure** — as a Reaction, reduce incoming damage by 2
2. **Press On** — ignore movement penalties and difficult terrain for 1 round
3. **Shake It Off** — remove one minor negative Effect currently affecting you (Minor Action)

### Nature
1. **Wilderness Sense** — detect hidden creatures, traps or hazards within 30' in natural environments
2. **Herbal Remedy** — use natural materials to heal a small amount of HP or cure a minor Effect (out of combat)
3. **Animal Empathy** — calm, distract or direct a natural animal (Wis attack vs Wis Defence)

### Stealth
1. **Feint** — cause an enemy to lose their Defence bonus against your next attack (Cha attack vs Wis Defence)
2. **Shadow Step** — move up to 15' without triggering Opportunity Attacks
3. **Pickpocket** — attempt to steal a small item from a target without being noticed (Dex vs Wis)

### Social
1. **Taunt** — force an enemy to direct their next attack at you rather than an ally (Cha attack vs Wis Defence)
2. **Inspire** — grant an ally +1 to their next roll (Minor Action)
3. **Read the Room** — gain insight into the emotional state or intent of everyone in a social encounter

### Academics
1. **Tactical Assessment** — study an enemy to identify a weakness; gain +1 to your next attack against them (Minor Action)
2. **Recall Lore** — make an immediate knowledge check about an enemy, object or situation
3. **Study Weakness** — after observing an enemy for 1 round, grant all allies +1 to attacks against that enemy

### Divine
1. **Sacred Strike** — a weapon attack that deals bonus radiant damage (uses Cha attack bonus)
2. **Lay on Hands** — touch to heal 1d4 + Wis bonus HP
3. **Turn Undead** — attempt to frighten undead creatures within 30' (Cha attack vs Wis Defence)

### Spiritual
1. **Focused Strike** — a melee attack using mental discipline; add Wis bonus to damage
2. **Ki Shield** — gain +1 to all Defence Targets until the start of your next turn (Minor Action)
3. **Mental Fortitude** — gain +2 to Wis Defence rolls until the start of your next turn (Reaction)

### Primal
1. **Reckless Attack** — attack with Advantage but your Defence Targets are reduced by 2 until your next turn
2. **Primal Scream** — intimidate all enemies within 20' (Str attack vs Wis Defence); on hit, target takes -1 to their next attack
3. **Savage Strike** — a raw, brutal melee attack; weapon damage + Con bonus

### Arcana
1. **Arcane Bolt** — a minor magical ranged attack (Int attack vs Wis Defence) for 1d6 damage
2. **Arcane Shield** — gain +1 Defence against magical attacks until the start of your next turn (Reaction)
3. **Detect Magic** — sense all magical auras within 30' (Minor Action)

### Occult
1. **Eldritch Bolt** — a dark ranged attack (Cha attack vs Con Defence) for 1d6 damage
2. **Hex** — mark an enemy; they take -1 to a chosen Attribute Defence until the end of the encounter (Minor Action)
3. **Dark Bargain** — spend 2 HP to gain +2 to any single roll (Free Action)

### Craft
1. **Quick Repair** — restore a damaged weapon or piece of armour to full function mid-combat (Minor Action)
2. **Improvised Weapon** — craft or repurpose any object as a weapon with no penalty
3. **Construct Command** — direct a mechanical construct or device to take an action on your behalf
