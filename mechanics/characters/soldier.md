# Soldier

*Portfolio of Arms*

The Soldier is the trained fighting professional of Avilund — the disciplined core of any company, whether holding a shield-wall, carrying the banner, or laying down fire from the line. Where others fight, the Soldier *soldiers*: drilled, equipped, and dependable. The three Paths carry that discipline in different directions — the **Vanguard** who will not be moved, the **Commander** who turns a mob into a unit, and the **Marksman** who ends a fight before it closes to arm's reach.

## Class Features

A Soldier brings **Strength** as their key Primary Attribute and the **Martial** ability Category, backed by a sturdy **Class HP of 3**. They are trained in the **Endurance** and **Intimidate** skills, in the **Heavy Blades, Polearms,** and **Crossbows** weapon groups, and in **Light Armour, Medium Armour,** and the **Light Shield**.

Your chosen **Path** adds your Secondary Attribute, a second ability Category, two more skills, and further proficiencies — see below.

## Paths

### Vanguard
The armoured anchor of the line. Vanguards hold ground, shield their comrades, and punish anything that tries to push through.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Secondary Attribute** | Constitution |
| **Category** | Protection |
| **Skills** | Survival, Perception |
| **Weapon Proficiencies** | Hammers/Maces, Spears/Lances |
| **Armour Proficiencies** | Heavy Armour, Heavy Shield |

*Note: Constitution never powers an attack, so a Vanguard's Protection attacks run off **Strength**; Constitution carries their defenses, HP, and saves — "Strength hits, Constitution endures."*

### Commander
The officer who fights by making everyone around them fight better — calling targets, rallying the line, and leading from the front with sabre and sidearm.

| **Skills** | Diplomacy, Perception |
| **Weapon Proficiencies** | Light Blades, Pistols |
| **Armour Proficiencies** | *(none beyond the Class)* |

### Marksman
The ranged specialist — bow, crossbow, or rifle — who controls the field from a distance and never lets the enemy dictate the range.

## Abilities

Every ability is a chassis you improve over a career. Most have one or more **ladders** — a base rank that comes with the ability, two rungs you buy with **Minor** Advances, and a top rung that costs a **Major**. Frequency (Daily → Encounter → At-Will) and Action cost are themselves ladders on many abilities.

### Martial *(Class)*

**Martial Strike** — *Offensive · the canvas*
Your bread-and-butter attack, and the one most shaped by your weapon.
- **Attack:** Strength vs AC · **1[W]**
- **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter → At-Will
- **Damage:** full ladder — +Str, die-size up (d4–d12), then ×2 (2[W])
- **Weapon Specialisation Feat hooks** (with the Feat + that weapon equipped):

| Weapon Group | Effect |
|---|---|
| Heavy Blades | +2 damage |
| Light Blades | +1 to hit |
| Hammers | Push 5' |
| Axes | Bleed 1 |
| Spears/Polearms | +5' reach |
| Flails/Chains | ignore the target's shield bonus to AC |
| Staves | +1 to one of your defenses until your next turn |
| Bows/Crossbows | may be made as a ranged attack (1×WRI) |

**Power Attack** — *Offensive · the heavy blow*
- **Attack:** Strength vs AC · **1[W]**
- **Damage ladder** (the specialty): up to **2[W] + Str**
- *Targets and Action cost are locked — pure single-target damage.*
- **Feat:** Heavy Blade or Hammer Specialisation → +2 damage (Hammer also Push 5')

**Defensive Strike** — *Offensive + Defensive*
- **Attack:** Strength vs AC · **1[W]**; on a hit, **+1 to one of your defenses** until your next turn
- **Ladder:** +1 → +2; further: longer duration, and "applies even on a miss" (via Feat)
- **Feat:** Shield Specialisation → +1 additional defense; Staff → shove the attacker / +defense vs them

**Parry** — *Defensive*
- **Action:** Move → Minor → Interrupt
- **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter
- **Effect ladder** (reduces the next incoming damage):

| Rank | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | −2 to the next incoming damage |
| 2 | −3 |
| 3 | −4 |
| 4 | −4 **and** make a Melee Basic Attack (riposte) |

- **Feat:** Light Blade Specialisation → riposte unlocks at Rank 3, and adds damage

**Disarming Strike** — *Debuff*
- **Attack:** Strength vs Armoured Dexterity · **1[W]**
- **Effect ladder:**

| Rank | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | target −1 to hit |
| 2 | −2 to hit |
| 3 | −2, and the target must spend a Minor action to re-grip its weapon |
| 4 | **Disarmed** — the weapon flies to an adjacent square |

- **Feat:** Chain weapon → on a Disarm, you grab and keep the weapon

**Martial Focus** — *Buff / setup*
- **Action:** Move → Minor → Free
- **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter
- **Effect ladder** (lasts only until your next attack):

| Rank | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | +1 to your next attack roll |
| 2 | +2 to your next attack roll |
| 3 | +2 to your next attack **and damage** roll |
| 4 | +2 to your next attack **and 2[W] damage** |

**Raise Shield** — *Defensive*
- **Requires** a shield equipped.
- **Action:** Move → Minor → Free
- **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter → At-Will
- **Effect:** while raised (until your next turn), apply your **shield's DR** to incoming attack damage — DR you do not get passively, only through this ability.

**Measure the Foe** — *Utility (non-combat)*
- **Action:** Minor (in combat) / instant (out of combat) · Range 30'
- **Effect:** learn a creature's HP tier and its highest Offense or softest Defense.
- **Ladder:** more targets · deeper information · greater range.

### Protection *(Vanguard)*

*Protection attacks run off **Strength**; Constitution carries the Vanguard's defenses and HP.*

**Shield Bash** — *Offensive · control*
- **Attack:** Strength vs AC · light/shield damage + **Daze** on hit
- **Action:** Move → Minor · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter
- **Effect:** the Daze (Action-Denial) ladder — R1 no Reactions/Interrupts → R2 + no Minor action → R3 + no Move action → R4 all actions
- **Feats:** Light Shield Specialisation → Push 5'; Heavy Shield Specialisation → Push 5' *or* Prone

**Marking Strike** — *Offensive*
The Martial Strike chassis, fitted for a defender: same attack and full Damage/Frequency scaling, but no weapon hooks — instead it always **Marks**.
- **Attack:** Strength vs AC · **1[W]** · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter → At-Will
- **Effect:** target is **Marked** (−1 to attack anyone but you), scaling up the Marked ladder

**Sentinel Strike** — *Offensive + Defensive*
- **Attack:** Strength vs AC · **1[W]** (delivers the buff on a hit) · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter → At-Will
- **Effect ladder** (a buff to **AC**):

| Rank | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | one ally +1 AC |
| 2 | you and one ally +1 AC |
| 3 | you and one ally +2 AC |
| 4 | you and **all adjacent allies** +2 AC |

- **Feat:** Light/Heavy Shield Specialisation → apply your Shield's DR to everyone this Effect is protecting

**Guard** — *Defensive*
- **Action:** Move → Minor → Interrupt
- **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter → At-Will (At-Will at Level 5)
- **Effect ladder:**

| Rank | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | +1 AC — self / 1 adjacent ally |
| 2 | +1 to all Armoured Defences — self / 1 adjacent ally |
| 3 | +2 to all Armoured Defences — self / 1 adjacent ally |
| 4 | +2 to all Armoured Defences — self / **all adjacent allies** |

- **Feat:** Light/Heavy Shield Specialisation → apply your Shield's DR to the protected

**Intercept** — *Defensive / maneuver*
- **Action:** Reaction · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter
- **Effect:** trade places with an adjacent ally, then —

| Rank | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | you take the damage |
| 2 | you take the damage **− your Armour DR** |
| 3 | the opponent must make the attack against **you**, resolved normally |
| 4 | as 3, and you may intercept for an ally up to 10' (2 squares) away |

- **Feat:** Light/Heavy Shield Specialisation → apply your Shield's DR

**Bulwark** — *Buff (self)*
- **Action:** Move → Minor · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter
- **Effect ladder:** 2 temp HP → 3 temp HP → 4 temp HP → **5 temp HP + +1 DR for 1 round**
- **Feats:** Medium Armour Specialisation → +2 temp HP; Heavy Armour Specialisation → +3 temp HP

**Stand Watch** — *Utility (non-combat)*
- **Frequency:** Daily
- **Effect ladder** (you cannot be surprised on your watch):

| Rank | Watch | Perception |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 2 hours | +1 |
| 2 | 4 hours | +1 |
| 3 | 6 hours | +1 |
| 4 | 8 hours | +2 |

### Leadership *(Commander)*

*Commands and buffs key off **Charisma**; the Commander's weapon attacks use Light Blades or Pistols.*

**Command** — *Offensive · action-grant*
- **Action:** Standard → Move → Minor · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter → At-Will
- **Effect ladder:**

| Rank | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | an ally makes a basic attack for free |
| 2 | a free basic attack, **+1 to hit** |
| 3 | a free basic attack, **+2 to hit** |
| 4 | an ally may take **any Standard action**; if it's an attack, +2 to hit |

**Commander's Strike** — *Offensive*
- **Attack:** Strength vs AC · **1[W]** · Standard only · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter → At-Will
- **Effect ladder** (all allies, versus the struck target, until next round):

| Rank | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | all allies +1 to hit the target |
| 2 | all allies +1 to hit **and damage** |
| 3 | all allies +2 to hit, +1 damage |
| 4 | all allies +2 to hit **and damage** |

**Focus Fire** — *Offensive*
The no-damage twin of Commander's Strike: no attack roll, just call the target — but a cheaper action.
- **Action:** Standard → Move → Minor · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter → At-Will
- **Effect ladder:** identical to Commander's Strike

**Resolute Strike** — *Offensive + Defensive*
- **Attack:** Melee *or* Ranged · **1[W]** · Standard only · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter → At-Will
- **Effect ladder** (on a hit, allies within 10' brace against the struck opponent):

| Rank | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | +1 AC vs that opponent's attacks of the type you used (melee/ranged) |
| 2 | +1 to all Defences vs that opponent |
| 3 | +1 to all Defences vs all opponents |
| 4 | +2 to all Defences vs all opponents |

- **Feats:** Pistol Specialisation → +1 to hit the opponent for 1 round; Light Blade Specialisation → the target is Marked

**Rally** — *Defensive*
- **Action:** Standard → Move → Minor · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter → At-Will
- **Effect ladder** (allies gain +1 to all defenses until your next turn; range widens):

| Rank | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | allies within 10' |
| 2 | allies within 20' |
| 3 | allies within 30' |
| 4 | allies within 30' **and 2 temp HP** |

**War Cry** — *Buff*
The offensive mirror of Rally.
- **Action:** Standard → Move → Minor → Free · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter → At-Will
- **Effect ladder** (allies gain +1 to hit on all attacks; range widens):

| Rank | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | allies within 10' |
| 2 | allies within 20' |
| 3 | allies within 30' |
| 4 | allies within 30' **and +2 damage** |

**Inspiring Word** — *Rally / heal*
- **Action:** Move → Minor · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter
- **Effect ladder:** an ally may attempt to shake a condition → with +1 → with +2 → with +2 **and gains 2 temp HP**
- **Targets ladder:** 1 ally ≤30' → 2 allies ≤30' → 3 allies ≤30' → **all allies within hearing range**

### Marksmanship *(Marksman)*

*All shots use **Dexterity** Offense on the Weapon Range Increment (WRI) track, with −0 / −2 / −4 penalties by band.*

**Marksman's Shot** — *Offensive · the canvas*
The ranged counterpart to Martial Strike.
- **Attack:** Dexterity vs AC · **W** · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter → At-Will
- **Damage:** full ladder (+Dex, die-size, ×2) · **Range:** increment ×1 → ×2 → ×3
- **Weapon Specialisation Feat hooks:**

| Weapon Group | Effect |
|---|---|
| Bows | +1 to hit |
| Crossbows | ignore the DR of armour |
| Slings | Push 5' |
| Thrown | +Str damage |
| Pistols | +2 damage within the first increment (point-blank) |
| Rifles | ignore range penalty |
| Grenades | hits a 5' burst |

**Pinning Shot** — *Offensive · control*
- **Attack:** Dexterity vs AC · **W** · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter → At-Will
- **Effect (Movement ladder):** −5' speed → −10' → −15' → **Immobilized**

**Skirmishing Shot** — *Offensive + Defensive*
- **Attack:** Dexterity vs AC · **W** · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter
- **Effect:** on a hit, shift 5' and gain +1 to a defense until your next turn (scales: shift 5'→10', defense +1→+2)

**Covering Fire** — *Defensive*
- **Action:** Interrupt only · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter
- **Trigger:** an enemy in range attacks an ally
- **Effect ladder:**

| Rank | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | −1 to that attack |
| 2 | −2 to that attack |
| 3 | −2 to that attack **and any other attacks from that target until the end of next round** |
| 4 | as 3, and also make a ranged basic attack against the target |

**Run and Gun** — *Movement*
- **Action:** Movement only · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter
- **Effect ladder:**

| Rank | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | move 5' and make a basic ranged attack |
| 2 | move 10' and make a basic ranged attack |
| 3 | move 15' and make a basic ranged attack |
| 4 | move 20' and take a Standard Action |

**Debilitating Shot** — *Debuff*
- **Attack:** Dexterity vs AC · **W** · Standard only · **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter
- **Effect (Flat Debuff ladder):**

| Rank | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | −1 to a chosen defense |
| 2 | −2 |
| 3 | −2 **+ Vulnerable 1** |
| 4 | −2 **+ Vulnerable 5** |

*(Vulnerable N = the target takes N extra damage vs a chosen damage type or a specific defense.)*

**Marksman's Eye** — *Utility (non-combat)*
- **Frequency:** Daily → Encounter
- **Effect ladder** (+1 on Perception rolls to spot things at range):

| Rank | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | spot things up to 50' away |
| 2 | up to 100' away |
| 3 | up to 200' away |
| 4 | +2, and up to 1000' away |
