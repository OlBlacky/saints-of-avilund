# Abilities

## Ability Types

There are three types of abilities:

**Universal** — available to all characters regardless of class. These do not need to be purchased. Examples: Melee Attack, Ranged Attack.

**Category** — tied to one of the 12 ability categories (Martial, Survival, etc.). Available to any character whose class includes that category. In-class characters pay standard AP costs; characters without that category cannot access them. Examples: abilities from the Martial or Survival category pools.

**Class** — tied to a specific class and also tagged with a category. Only characters who own that class may purchase them, regardless of category overlap. They cost the same as Category abilities for in-class characters. Examples: Power Strike (Martial · Soldier only).

> The category tag on a Class ability indicates its flavour and discipline — it does not grant access to characters who merely share that category.

---

## Ability Categories

Each class has 2 primary categories. There are 12 categories in total:

| Category | Description |
|---|---|
| **Martial** | Combat, weapons, fighting techniques |
| **Survival** | Endurance, hardship, staying alive |
| **Nature** | Wilderness, animals, the natural world |
| **Stealth** | Hiding, deception, subterfuge |
| **Social** | Persuasion, manipulation, influence |
| **Academics** | Knowledge, lore, study |
| **Divine** | Faith, holy power, sacred calling |
| **Spiritual** | Inner discipline, mind over body |
| **Primal** | Raw instinct, savage power, ancient forces |
| **Arcana** | Studied or innate arcane power |
| **Occult** | Forbidden knowledge, dark pacts |
| **Craft** | Making, building, inventing |

---

## Ability Pools

| Pool | Type | Refresh |
|---|---|---|
| **Special Pool** | Class Special abilities | Short Rest |
| **Main Pool** | Class Main abilities | Long Rest |

- Starting Special Pool = 1 (granted when you purchase the class)
- Starting Main Pool = 1 (granted when you purchase your first Main ability)
- Each class has its own independent pools
- Pools can be expanded with AP (see [Advancement Points](../characters/advancement-points.md))

## Ability Card Format

All abilities are presented in a standard card format.

---
**[ABILITY NAME]**
*[Universal / Category / Class] · [Category] · [Class — if applicable]*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Action** | Standard Action / Minor Action / Reaction / Full Round Action |
| **Range** | Melee / distance in feet |
| **Attack** | [Attribute] vs [Attribute] Defence *(notes)* |
| **Duration** | Instant / 1 round / etc. |

**Base:** Description of the core effect that applies at all tiers.

*Tier effects are cumulative.*

| Tier | Effect |
|---|---|
| **1** | Base |
| **2** | — |
| **3** | — |
| **4** | — |
| **5** | — |

---

> **Conditions** referenced on ability cards (e.g. Bleed, Grapple, Prone) use a capital letter and refer to entries in the Conditions document. Any value after the name (e.g. Bleed 1d4) is the variable for that condition instance.

## Universal Abilities

Available to all characters at no AP cost.

| Ability | Action | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Melee Attack** | Standard | Standard weapon attack against an adjacent target |
| **Ranged Attack** | Standard | Standard ranged weapon attack |

## Category Abilities

The following are example abilities from each category. Full ability cards to be developed.

### Martial
1. **Power Strike** *(Class — Soldier)* — see [Soldier Abilities](soldier-abilities.md)
2. **Defensive Strike** *(Class — Soldier)* — see [Soldier Abilities](soldier-abilities.md)
3. **Disarming Strike** — melee attack attempting to knock an opponent's weapon away (Str vs Str Defence)

### Survival
1. **Endure** *(Class — Soldier)* — see [Soldier Abilities](soldier-abilities.md)
2. **Press On** — ignore movement penalties and difficult terrain for 1 round
3. **Shake It Off** — remove one minor negative Condition currently affecting you (Minor Action)

### Nature
1. **Wilderness Sense** — detect hidden creatures, traps or hazards within 30' in natural environments
2. **Herbal Remedy** — use natural materials to heal a small amount of HP or cure a minor Condition (out of combat)
3. **Animal Empathy** — calm, distract or direct a natural animal (Wis vs Wis Defence)

### Stealth
1. **Feint** — cause an enemy to lose their Defence bonus against your next attack (Cha vs Wis Defence)
2. **Shadow Step** — move up to 15' without triggering Opportunity Attacks
3. **Pickpocket** — attempt to steal a small item from a target without being noticed (Dex vs Wis)

### Social
1. **Taunt** — force an enemy to direct their next attack at you rather than an ally (Cha vs Wis Defence)
2. **Inspire** — grant an ally +1 to their next roll (Minor Action)
3. **Read the Room** — gain insight into the emotional state or intent of everyone in a social encounter

### Academics
1. **Tactical Assessment** — study an enemy to identify a weakness; gain +1 to your next attack against them (Minor Action)
2. **Recall Lore** — make an immediate knowledge check about an enemy, object or situation
3. **Study Weakness** — after observing an enemy for 1 round, grant all allies +1 to attacks against that enemy

### Divine
1. **Sacred Strike** — a weapon attack that deals bonus radiant damage (Cha Attack)
2. **Lay on Hands** — touch to heal 1d4 + Wis bonus HP
3. **Turn Undead** — attempt to frighten undead creatures within 30' (Cha vs Wis Defence)

### Spiritual
1. **Focused Strike** — a melee attack using mental discipline; add Wis bonus to damage
2. **Ki Shield** — gain +1 to all Defence Targets until the start of your next turn (Minor Action)
3. **Mental Fortitude** — gain +2 to Wis Defence until the start of your next turn (Reaction)

### Primal
1. **Reckless Attack** — attack with Advantage but Defence Targets are reduced by 2 until your next turn
2. **Primal Scream** — intimidate all enemies within 20' (Str vs Wis Defence); on hit, target takes −1 to their next attack
3. **Savage Strike** — a raw, brutal melee attack; weapon damage + Con bonus

### Arcana
1. **Arcane Bolt** — a ranged magical attack (Int vs Wis Defence) for 1d6 damage
2. **Arcane Shield** — gain +1 Defence against magical attacks until the start of your next turn (Reaction)
3. **Detect Magic** — sense all magical auras within 30' (Minor Action)

### Occult
1. **Eldritch Bolt** — a dark ranged attack (Cha vs Con Defence) for 1d6 damage
2. **Hex** — mark an enemy; they take −1 to a chosen attribute Defence until the end of the encounter (Minor Action)
3. **Dark Bargain** — spend 2 HP to gain +2 to any single roll (Free Action)

### Craft
1. **Quick Repair** — restore a damaged weapon or piece of armour to full function mid-combat (Minor Action)
2. **Improvised Weapon** — use any object as a weapon with no penalty
3. **Construct Command** — direct a mechanical construct or device to take an action on your behalf
