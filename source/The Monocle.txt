The Monocle
This newspaper was established in 1643 by a man named Ghesthaller Ravenskreig a foreigner from Dunstanmoore. The paper originally published a monthly accounting of the goings-on of the well-to-do of the City, but over the years, as paper has become cheaper (especially with the recent boom in the lumber trade), and more common people are reading the paper, its focus has changed a little more towards common stories and trashier subjects. It now publishes 5 papers during the week, with a weekend edition on Sunday that has both a compilation of the best stories from the week, and new material. The weekly paper sells for 2d, while the thicker weekend edition sells for 5d.
