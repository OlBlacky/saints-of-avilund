---
title: Character Creation
summary: How to build a character in Saints of Avilund — the short version.
---

Building a character is a matter of spending **Advances** — the universal currency of growth — against the menu your Class and Path open up. Here is the short version.

## What you start with

Every character begins at **Level 0** with **11 Major Advances** and **11 Minor Advances** to spend, and a base of **5 Hit Points**. All six attributes — Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma — begin at **+0**.

## Step 1 · Choose a Class and Path

Your **Class** sets your **Primary Attribute**, one **Ability Category**, your **Class HP**, two **Class Skills**, and a set of weapon and armour **proficiencies**. Your **Path** adds your **Secondary Attribute**, a second Category, two more skills, and further proficiencies.

Your Primary and Secondary are your two **key attributes** — the only ones that can ever climb past +2.

## Step 2 · Spend your 11 Major Advances

Major Advances mostly buy **attribute points** and **Abilities**.

- **Attributes.** Each new point costs more than the last: **+1** costs 1 Major, **+2** costs 3 in total, **+3** costs 6 in total. At Level 0 no attribute may exceed **+2** — your two key attributes can reach +3 later, +4 at 5th level, and +5 at 10th.
- **Abilities.** Each Ability costs **1 Major** and must come from one of your two Categories. Every Ability is bought at a frequency of **once per day**; at creation you may spend one Major to raise a single Ability to **once per encounter**.

## Step 3 · Spend your 11 Minor Advances

Minor Advances buy the finer details — each on the same rising cost as attributes (+1 costs 1, +2 costs 3):

- **+1 to an Offense** (your attack bonus with an attribute) or **+1 to a Defense**.
- **Hit Points** — each Minor adds your **Class HP** (1, 2, or 3).
- **Skill ranks** — +1 in any skill, or +2 in a **Class Skill**.
- **Proficiencies, Feats, and languages.**

Offenses, defenses, and skill ranks all stack *on top of* the governing attribute.

## Step 4 · Take a Flaw (optional)

You may lower up to **two** attributes by −1 at creation, gaining **1 extra Major Advance** for each.

## Your numbers

Once everything is spent, note your derived statistics:

- **Offense** (attack bonus) = attribute + offense ranks.
- **Defense** (target number) = 10 + attribute + defense ranks; **Armoured** defenses also add your armour. Your **AC** is your Armoured Constitution Defense.
- **Hit Points** = 5 + the HP you bought.

## Afterwards · advancement

You climb from Level 0 to a cap of **Level 11**. Each level is three **milestones** — roughly one per adventure — and every milestone grants **+1 Major and +1 Minor Advance**, spent exactly as above. Your character sharpens one notch at a time, adventure by adventure.
