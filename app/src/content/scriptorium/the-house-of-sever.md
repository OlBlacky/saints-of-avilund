*Avitian Severus is the name commonly given to the father of Saint Avitus by historians. Virtually nothing is known of this man save for his brief mention as "The Saint's Father" in The Tale of the Woodland King. Even less is known of the mother of Saint Avitus, though it is strict dogma in the Faith that she was Celestial in origin.

**Avitus and Ruth had various other children who did not survive into adulthood, or whose names have escaped historical record

*** Canaan's existence and parentage is highly disputed and controversial. Several obscure sources cite Muriel as his mother, and among those some even suggest that Canaan's father was Gaul. However, conventional theories suggest that this incestuous relationship was wholly invented in relatively recent times solely for the purpose of establishing a blood link between Avitus and Saint Ulric. Virtually nothing is none of Canaan, and his presence on this chart is almost certainly an exploitation of that mystery by the scholars of Saint Ulric.
