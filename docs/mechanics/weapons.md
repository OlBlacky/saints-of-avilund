# Weapons

## Weapon Traits

Every weapon has the following traits:

| Trait | Description |
|---|---|
| **Weapon Group** | Determines proficiency and specialisation (e.g. Medium Blades, Heavy Hammers) |
| **Type** | Piercing, Slashing, or Blunt |
| **Damage** | Die rolled on a hit |
| **Hands** | One-handed (1H) or Two-handed (2H) |
| **Weight** | In lbs — also determines group tier: < 2 lbs = Light, 2–5 lbs = Medium, > 5 lbs = Heavy |
| **Cost** | In-world price *(costs to be revised once currency is defined)* |
| **Range** | *(Ranged and Thrown weapons only)* Three increments in feet |
| **Special Properties** | See below |

### Range Penalties
| Increment | Penalty |
|---|---|
| 1st | No penalty |
| 2nd | −2 to attack |
| 3rd | −4 to attack |
| Beyond 3rd | Out of range |

---

## Special Properties

| Property | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Finesse** | May use Dex instead of Str for attack and damage rolls |
| **Reach** | Melee range is 10' instead of 5' |
| **Thrown** | May be used as a ranged weapon; has range increments |
| **Versatile** | May be used one- or two-handed; two-handed increases the damage die by one step |
| **Loading** | Must spend a Minor Action to reload between shots |
| **Concealable** | May be hidden on the body |
| **Defensive** | Grants +1 to a Defence Target while wielded |
